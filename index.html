About

I'm a final-year BSc Information Technology student at North-West University, working across software development, database design and computer networking. I like problems that have a correct structure hiding somewhere inside them — a normal form, a subnet, a proof — and I enjoy the work of finding it.

Day to day that means writing in Python, Java, C#, C++ and SQL, applying core software development principles, and building things that are meant to be maintained, not just demoed. I'm dedicated and disciplined, I work well in a team, and I've been recognised for leadership both inside and outside the classroom throughout my studies.

Outside of a screen, I train — cycling, running and swimming — because endurance sport teaches the same lesson software does: consistency beats intensity, and the plan matters more than the motivation.
